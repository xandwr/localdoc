# Funding & Roadmap



> **Building sustainable infrastructure for developer documentation**

> Doctown is funded by people who’d rather build public tech than complain about private ones.


Doctown isn't funded by VCs. It's funded by developers who believe documentation should be a public good, not a walled garden.

If a thousand of us throw five bucks in the same hat, we can build the kind of infrastructure no VC would ever touch. No dark patterns. No "engagement funnels." No ads whispering that you're the product.

Just clean, permanent, human-owned technology.

---



## 💡 The Model
Every dollar goes somewhere visible. A slice keeps the servers humming. A slice keeps me alive long enough to keep building.

The rest goes right back into the commons—funding doc archiving, contributor bounties, and open tools that everyone can use.



**LocalDoc CLI**: Free and open source, forever.

**Doctown Registry**: Freemium SaaS that scales with the community.

### Free Tier (Unlimited Users)- No lock-in schemes.

✅ Install unlimited docpacks  
✅ Browse and search the registry  
✅ Use LocalDoc CLI with full features  
✅ Publish up to 3 public docpacks  

Choose permanence over perks. Choose a future that doesn’t delete itself when funding dries up.
**Cost to us**: ~$0.002 per user/month (bandwidth only)

### Pro Tier ($5/month)
✅ Everything in Free  
✅ Publish unlimited docpacks  
✅ Verified badge (GitHub-linked)   
✅ Analytics for your docpacks  
✅ Priority indexing  
✅ Custom docpack URLs
